Yesterday´s Dream

Author of Story:
José Alejandro Zavala Manjarrez

Link of the Photo:
The Photo

The content about the story: “In the beautiful time of the year called "Summer", in which I did not stop enjoying those beautiful memories in which she enveloped me, maybe she also thought the same as me. We, on the beach, where we could not stop thinking about our dreams and our vision of things. When we see the sunrise, I suppose it is always a good moment, we talked about ourselves, about what each other felt, in which we would lean on to face the things that come our way. After that day, the usual routine, since she is my neighbor, because she came to my house to wake me up, although I also went to her house to wake her up if she did not get up before her. We may be very strange because of this, but honestly, we loved it. Although there is something that she did not know how to do, make breakfast in the morning. I cooked her scrambled eggs with chorizo ​​and some omelets to complement the dish, because I always saw her jumping for joy at having that type of breakfast, since she is not from this country, but from the United States, although I must admit that her name In part it is not from that place, it is called "Lisa Nanase", although I understand "Lisa", but the "Nanase" is Japanese, I suppose that parents did not want to leave their own children without a name that defines them. Although she is related to the Japanese, she is blonde, tall, well defined physically and with blue eyes, that's right, her typical eyes she has too. That is why she likes this type of food very much, as if it were magical for her, although I must admit that this type of food will not help her in the future. Well, I make myself some scrambled eggs, so as not to have such a heavy breakfast, although she doesn't need to do that type of diet, she does a good exercise. Well, as expected, finishing breakfast on our way to school, holding hands, maybe without knowing it, we are someone who could be classified as "Couple". Although without knowing it, this will not be a good day for anyone.”
